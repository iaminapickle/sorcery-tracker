"Bottom" can have two different meanings in *Sorcery*, depending on context.

When referring to your deck, bottom or bottommost simply means the bottom card of the deck. For example, [[Autumn River]].

When referring to the realm, bottom means the row closest to you. For example, [[Polar Explorers]].
