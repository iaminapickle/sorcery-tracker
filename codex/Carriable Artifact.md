A carriable artifact is any artifact that's not a [[Artifact#Monument|monument]] and not a minion (i.e. not an ((automaton). Carriable artifacts have additional options when being cast as a spell. See "Casting Artifacts" for more details, and "Ownership and Control))" for details on how control can change for carriable artifacts.
