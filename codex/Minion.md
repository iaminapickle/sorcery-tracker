Minions are your greatest allies, assisting you with myriad abilities in offense and defense. [[Plumed Pegasus]] is an example of a minion.

Minions have characteristics, specifically [[Characteristics#Minion Characteristics|minion characteristics]], and are one of the six card types in the game.

The "[[Casting Spells#Casting Minions|Casting Minions]]" entry has details on how to cast minion spells.
