Magics are transient spells that have an immediate impact but do not persist in the realm. [[Heat Ray]] is an example of a magic.

Magics have characteristics, specifically [[Characteristics#Magic Characteristics|magic characteristics]], and are one of the six card types in the game.

The "[[Casting Spells#Casting Magics|Casting Magics]]" entry has details on how to cast magic spells.
